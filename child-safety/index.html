Child Safety Standards

Last updated: March 2026

Clap-Serv is a service marketplace platform that connects customers with local service providers. We are committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users and take child safety extremely seriously.

Our Commitment

Clap-Serv has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We strictly prohibit any content, behavior, or activity on our platform that sexually exploits or endangers minors in any way.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on Clap-Serv:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors
  • Any content that sexualizes individuals under the age of 18
  • Sharing, distributing, or linking to CSAE material
  • Any communication intended to exploit or harm children

Enforcement

We actively enforce these standards through the following measures:

  • Immediate removal of any reported CSAE content
  • Permanent suspension of accounts found in violation
  • Reporting confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant law enforcement authorities
  • Cooperation with law enforcement investigations related to child safety

Age Restrictions

Clap-Serv is intended for users aged 18 and above. Users who are found to be under the age of 18 will have their accounts removed.

Reporting

If you encounter any content or behavior on Clap-Serv that you believe violates our child safety standards, please report it immediately. All reports are treated with urgency and confidentiality.

Designated Contact

For child safety concerns, reports, or inquiries related to CSAE, please contact our designated safety point of contact:

Child Safety Contact
Email: contact@clap-serv.com
We aim to respond to all safety reports within 24 hours.

Updates to This Policy

We may update these child safety standards as our platform evolves or as regulations change. Any significant updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
